Common Topics

Subscription & Billing

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions to view, modify, or cancel.

iCloud Sync Issues

Make sure iCloud is enabled for PhantomScan in Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Apps Using iCloud. Sync may take a few moments.

Export Problems

If you're having trouble exporting, please tell us the file type and destination app. Screenshots help us diagnose the issue faster.

Face ID / Passcode

You can enable or disable biometric lock in the app settings. If Face ID isn't working, try re-enabling it in Settings ‚Üí Face ID & Passcode.

Restore Purchases

If your subscription isn't recognized, go to app settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID.

Feature Requests

We love hearing your ideas! Email us your suggestions and we'll consider them for future updates.

Typical response time: within 24 hours

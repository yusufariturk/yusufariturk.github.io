We believe in data minimization. The information we collect is strictly limited to what is necessary for the app's functionality:

Data Security

Security is at the heart of PhantomScan:

  • Zero Knowledge: We do not upload your documents to our servers. We cannot decrypt or view your files even if we wanted to.
